About Us

We are the Jedi Council,

The Jedi Council was founded on October 31, 2022, by Benji Warburton, Jake Bowie, and Jedi Podeesamer. We are an organisation whose aim is to bring to light the issues surrounding technology such as digital divide, problems concerning design for reuse and repair, and the regulation of large and dominant technological companies.
