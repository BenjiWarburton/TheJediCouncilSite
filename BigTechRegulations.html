Regulation of Big Tech

Regulating the technology industry

“Big Tech” such as Facebook, Apple, Amazon, Netflix, and Google have grown significantly dominant and influential in the past few years. These five technology giants are among the largest companies in the world, with a combined market capitalisation of around $7 trillion as of Q1 2022 which is approximately twice the GDP of the United Kingdom. The success of these companies has made them a staple in the technological world, but are they doing more harm than good?
